Cheese Cake

Description

This delicious cheesecake is made with a creamy filling and a graham cracker crust. It's the perfect dessert for any occasion!

Serve it chilled, topped with fresh berries or a drizzle of chocolate sauce for an extra special touch.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add 1 cup sugar and beat until well combined.
  4. Add the vanilla extract and eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Finally, mix in the sour cream until smooth.
  5. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  6. Bake for 1 hour, or until the center is set and the edges are lightly browned.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
